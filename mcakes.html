Mauritian Street food

About Mauritian Street food

Maurtian Street Food has always a special place in all Mauritians' heart.
These cheap and tasty dishes are a considered as the world's 8th wonder; specially at tea time with a good tea!

Dishes

Gateaux Piments

Gateaux Piments are the best cakes Mauritians enjoy. Be it with bread and butter or in a curry or on the go, definitely a must!
These can be found at the corner of every street and market days.
Recipe can be found on : Gateaux Piments recipe

Samoussa

Samoussa are awesome, each can bring a different filling; meat, cheese or the classic potato curry. Samoussas are best enjoyed with chilli sauce.
These can be found at the corner of every street and market days.
Recipe can be found on : Samoussa recipe

Dholl puri

Dholl puri is one of the best dishes in Mauritian history. Filled with dholl, freshly cooked and served with curry, for less than rs20, this delicacy is truly a must try!
These can be found at the corner of every street and market days.
Recipe can be found on : Dholl puri recipe

Boulettes

Freshly steamed, served with chilli paste, for less than rs60 a serving, this dish gives value to healthy food and street food!
These can be found at the corner of every street and fusion cuisine restaurants.
Recipe can be found on : Boulettes recipe
