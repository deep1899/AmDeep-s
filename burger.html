Burgers

About burgers

A dish consisting of a flat round cake of minced beef, or sometimes another savoury meat, that is fried or grilled.

It is served in a split bun or roll with various condiments and toppings. In Mauritius, there are many restaurants which provide this delicious dish and you'll find the best ones below!

Restaurants

McDonald's

The golden arches never fail to impress! With a variety of burgers, drinks, desserts and their famous fries, McDonald's will always be the people's choice for fast and tasty meals!
You'll find the outlets at Port Louis - Riche Terre - Grand Baie - Flacq - Ebene - Phoenix - Beau Bassin - Valentina - Moka - Cascavelle - Tamarin - Trianon.

The Drive

Their homemade patties are grilled to order and served on a buttery toasted bun with our signature sauce.
The patties are made from 100% pure beef, chicken or lamb meat with the finest quality seasonings and
they also offer an array of drinks ranging from homemade ice-teas to smoothies!
Available at Vacoas, Mont Choisy and Ebene.

Seb's Kitchen

Enjoy freshly prepared juicy, tender burgers with the toppings of your choice! Seb's is available at Beau Bassin and Grand Baie!

Nina's Burgers

A true delight to explore, especially if you are around the north. The burgers will definitely surprise you and totally value for money!
