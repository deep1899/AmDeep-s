About Mauritian (Creole cuisine) food

The creole cuisine is eaten by every Mauritians and has its influences from African, Indian, and French cuisine.

Creole cuisine in Mauritian include dishes and condiments such as: Creole rougaille (a spicy tomato sauce with meat or fish) which shows African heritage of the dish. In Mauritius, there are many restaurants which provide this delicious dish and you'll find the best ones below!
