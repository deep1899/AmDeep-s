Mauritian Desserts and Drinks

About Desserts

Maurtian Desserts have always been a quick bite, be it cold, sweet, hot or salty!

Dishes

Vona Corona

Served with pineapple jelly and coconut paste, Mauritians love this dessert.
With a variety of flavours, the dessert can be found on cornerstreets, shopping malls, and market days.
