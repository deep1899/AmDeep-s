About pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat round base of dough baked with a topping of tomatoes and cheese.

This dish is typically prepared with added meat, or vegetables. In Mauritius, there are many restaurants which provide this delicious dish and you'll find the best ones below!
